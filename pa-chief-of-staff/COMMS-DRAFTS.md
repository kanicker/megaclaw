# COMMS-DRAFTS.md — Executive Communication Templates

These are reusable skeletons. Adapt tone and length to context.

---

## Quick update to peers

**Subject:** Quick update on [topic]

- Where we are:
- What changed:
- What I need from you:
- Timing:

---

## Nudge to a direct report

**Subject:** Checking in on [deliverable]

- Wanted to check in on [deliverable] due [date].
- What is the current status?
- Any blockers I can remove?

---

## Ask for a decision

**Subject:** Decision needed: [decision] by [date]

- Context (2-3 sentences):
- Options:
- Recommendation:
- Risk if delayed:

---

## One-page decision memo

**Title:** [Decision]

- **Goal:** What we're trying to achieve
- **Success criteria:** How we'll know it worked
- **Options:** (2-3, with tradeoffs for each)
- **Recommendation:** Which option and why
- **Risks and mitigations:**
- **Reversibility:** Can we undo this? At what cost?
- **Comms plan:** Who needs to know, when, how
